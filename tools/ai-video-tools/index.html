Building a Full AI Video Workflow

  1. Script: Write your script with ChatGPT or Claude. Use the prompt: "Write a 60-second explainer script for [topic], aimed at [audience]. Use short sentences. Each sentence should work as a standalone caption."
  2. Visuals: Generate b-roll footage with Sora or Runway from your script sections. For talking-head videos, use HeyGen to create your AI avatar presenter.
  3. Voiceover: Record your own voice or generate AI voiceover with ElevenLabs. For HeyGen avatar videos, the lip-sync voiceover is built in.
  4. Edit: Assemble in CapCut. Add auto-captions, music, and transitions. Use the AI voice enhancement to clean up any audio issues.
  5. Publish: Export in the correct format for your platform — 9:16 for TikTok and Reels, 16:9 for YouTube, 1:1 for LinkedIn.

Which Tool Should You Start With?

  • You want to generate video from text descriptions: Start with Sora if you have ChatGPT Plus, or Runway's free tier.
  • You want to create presenter videos without being on camera: HeyGen is purpose-built for this.
  • You make YouTube or podcast content and need voiceover: ElevenLabs free tier covers most individual creators.
  • You create short-form social content: CapCut AI is free and faster than every alternative.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best AI video generator in 2026?
For text-to-video quality, Sora (OpenAI) produces the most realistic footage. For creative film-style content, Runway Gen-3 Alpha is the professional choice. For AI avatar presenter videos, HeyGen leads the market.
Can I create videos entirely with AI — no filming required?
Yes. A complete workflow exists: script with ChatGPT, visuals with Sora or Runway, voiceover with ElevenLabs, editing with CapCut. The result is a fully produced video without any camera, microphone, or studio.
Are AI video tools free?
Several offer meaningful free tiers. Runway gives 125 free credits. ElevenLabs gives 10,000 characters per month free. CapCut AI is free for most features. Sora requires ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo). HeyGen gives one free video.
Can viewers tell AI video from real video?
For text-to-video content, trained viewers can often spot AI generation, particularly in hands and complex motion. For AI voiceover (ElevenLabs) and AI avatars (HeyGen), the quality has reached a level where most viewers cannot distinguish from human-produced content in casual viewing.
Which AI video tool is best for YouTube?
For YouTube, the most effective workflow is: ChatGPT for scripting, ElevenLabs for voiceover, Sora or Runway for b-roll, and CapCut or DaVinci Resolve for final editing. This produces polished content at a fraction of traditional production cost.
