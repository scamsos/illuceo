How to Get the Most from Free AI Tools

  1. Rotate between tools — Claude Free and ChatGPT Free both have daily limits. Using both doubles your effective free usage.
  2. Use specific prompts — Free models perform better with detailed instructions. Vague prompts waste your limited credits.
  3. Save your best prompts — When you find a prompt that works well, save it. Reusing effective prompts multiplies your output without using extra credits.
  4. Upgrade strategically — Only pay for a tool you use daily. If you use it three times a week, the free tier is probably enough.

Frequently Asked Questions
