Our Mission

The word illuceo comes from Latin — it means "to begin giving light, to dawn upon the mind." That is exactly what we set out to do every single day.

Artificial intelligence is reshaping every profession, every industry, every aspect of daily life. But most AI coverage is either too technical for non-specialists, too shallow for professionals, or buried inside newsletters requiring subscriptions. ILLUCEO exists to fix that.

We publish daily news, in-depth tutorials, tool comparisons, and profession-specific guides — all in plain language, all free, all in your language.

Editorial Standards

Every piece of content published on ILLUCEO follows these principles:

  • Accuracy first. We verify claims before publishing. When information is uncertain, we say so.
  • No sponsored headlines. Advertisers and affiliate partners never influence our editorial coverage. Reviews are honest.
  • Transparency on AI use. We use AI to assist with research and drafting, always with human review and editorial judgment applied.
  • Freshness. Articles are updated when information changes. We show "Last Updated" dates prominently.
  • Depth over clicks. We write for comprehension, not outrage. Every article aims to leave you genuinely smarter.

How We Make Money

ILLUCEO is free and will stay free. We generate revenue through:

  • Affiliate partnerships — when you click a tool link and subscribe, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This is disclosed on every relevant page.
  • Display advertising — standard display ads served by Google AdSense on content pages.
  • Sponsored content — clearly labeled sponsored posts from AI companies. Never disguised as editorial.

Our affiliate relationship with NordVPN is disclosed prominently throughout the site. We recommend NordVPN because we believe in internet privacy, not because they pay us — though they do, and we are transparent about that.
